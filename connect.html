We’re Glad You’re Here.


The OpenLab is a fresh new resource at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago.

The Open Lab is dedicated to promoting and supporting open art and design practices, principles and initiatives at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago.

It’s a place for creatives to explore and expand open practices, face-to-face and online. Check in often to see what we’re up, and stay in touch!
